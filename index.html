Welcome!

Welcome to my own personal site! I like to use this site to code more casually, using what I learned to make things exist without needing to make them fully functional. At the moment I'm not taking online commissions, however I am preparing to open them up again in the coming weeks with an updated price tag and terms of service so stay tuned for that!


...Oh in case you're new here, my commissions usually involve creating blocky 3D models and blocky art like you'd see out of old video game consoles. I found that there are people out there that seem to like that sort of stuff, so I figured it would be a good idea to cater to some of those people. If you still don't know what I'm talking about, I mean things like this:



Plus there are new games out there that even today go out of their way to make their games look like they came from an older console and are frequently praised for capturing the style incredibly well or for going their own route with that particular style. Some of those games include Shovel Knight, Tunic, Animal Well, A Short Hike, Dead Cells, Pseudoregalia, Pipistrello and the Cursed Yoyo, Cavern of Dreams, and various other games that I am likely missing. To be honest I grew up with consoles like the Game Boy Advance, Nintendo 64 and a Wii filled with various Virtual Console games so I have a bit of fondness to these sorts of games as well. I've also studied the inner workings of games and learned of various techniques that they've used in order to accomplish certain effects, to save space or to make the game have a better feel for the player so I have a greater respect and appreciation for older games, good or bad, and the developers who made these games.


Anyhow it seems making that sort of art for other people does seem to make them happy to an extent, so if it really does make them happy then I think that's a good reason to keep going forward with this.
